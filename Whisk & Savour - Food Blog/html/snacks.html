Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
3. In a large bowl, combine the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar. Beat until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
4. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs. Beat until well combined.
5. Gradually add the flour mixture, mixing until just combined.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
7. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing about 2 inches apart.
8. Bake until the cookies are golden around the edges but still soft in the center, 10 to 12 minutes.
9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

Chips Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly using a mandoline or sharp knife.
2. Place the potato slices in a large bowl of cold water and let them soak for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
3. Drain and rinse the potatoes, then pat them dry with a clean towel.
4. In a large pot or deep fryer, heat vegetable oil to 350째F (175째C).
5. Fry the potato slices in batches, being careful not to overcrowd the pot. Fry until golden and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
6. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the chips to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
7. Immediately season the chips with salt while they are still hot.
8. Let the chips cool slightly before serving.

Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, or mixed berries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup almond milk or any milk of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)

Instructions:

1. Peel the banana and break it into chunks.
2. In a blender, combine the banana, frozen berries, Greek yogurt, orange juice, and almond milk.
3. Blend until smooth, about 1-2 minutes.
4. Taste and add honey or maple syrup if desired, then blend again to combine.
5. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
